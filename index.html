About Us

Particle Technologies India is a reputable company that specializes in supplying high-quality analytical instruments and providing analytical testing services. We collaborate closely with leading manufacturers from around the globe to ensure that our customers have access to the latest advancements and best-in-class technologies.Our comprehensive product portfolio covers a vast array of analytical techniques, including particle size analysis, surface area analysis, particle counting, zeta potential measurement, and more.

At Particle Technologies India, we understand the critical role that accurate and precise analytical equipment plays in various sectors, including pharmaceuticals, chemicals, food processing, environmental monitoring, and more. Our comprehensive range of equipment is designed to meet the diverse needs of our valued customers.

As a leading provider of analytical testing solutions, we offer a comprehensive range of services that deliver accurate results and actionable insights. Our state-of-the-art laboratory is equipped with advanced technology and operated by a team of highly skilled scientists and technicians dedicated to delivering excellence in testing. Whether you require testing for pharmaceuticals, chemicals, environmental samples, or food and beverages, we have the expertise to provide reliable and timely results. Our analytical testing services span a wide range of applications, including but not limited to method development and validation, product development support, stability testing, impurity analysis, and quality control.

We adhere to stringent quality standards and regulatory requirements, ensuring that our customers receive accurate, reliable, and compliant results. Customer satisfaction is at the core of our business philosophy, and we strive to build long-lasting relationships with our clients based on trust, reliability, and exceptional service. We understand that every customer has unique needs, and we tailor our solutions to meet those requirements, providing customized packages that align with their budget and application demands.
